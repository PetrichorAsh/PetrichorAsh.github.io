About Me

My research focuses on oil spill detection in port optical imagery and offshore SAR remote sensing images, with particular interests in semantic segmentation, SAR/optical image analysis, vision foundation model fine-tuning, and computer vision-based visual perception. I am especially interested in developing robust deep learning methods for complex maritime scenes, including weak-boundary oil spill segmentation, multi-source remote sensing interpretation, and foundation model adaptation for marine environmental monitoring.
